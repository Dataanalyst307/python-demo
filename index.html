Weekend plans

Keep this weekend light and intentional. Start Saturday slowly with breakfast at home, then stop by the market for flowers, fruit, and a fresh loaf of bread. The goal is to avoid overbooking the day and leave room for a little spontaneity.

In the afternoon, take care of the practical errands first: drop off the return package, pick up coffee beans, and restock a few basics for the kitchen. If there is still time, spend an hour reorganizing the desk so Monday starts clean instead of rushed.

Saturday evening is reserved for dinner with Maya at 7:30. Wear something simple, leave the house early, and keep the rest of the night open. No extra plans afterward. A quiet walk home would be enough.

Sunday should stay mostly offline. Read for a while, prep lunches for the week, and review the calendar before the evening. Finish by writing down three priorities for Monday so the next day begins with clarity.
